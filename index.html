Story

傳承技藝，融合創新 繼往開來是我們品牌的核心價值 將傳統的工藝技術融合了品牌的創新思維 打造出屬於每位紳士的專屬西服。

Choose from a variety of fabrics including English, Italian, and Japanese in either a classic suit or a more creative approach, adhere to quality garment and traditional tailoring to create unique apparel tailored to you.

Steps to Style

01 Inquiry

透過專業的諮詢，我們將與您一同找出最合適的風格。依不同的場合、用途，量身打造出專屬於您的手工西服

Communication plays big part of whole process, with Gaute professional team, we can find out the best types of suit tjat suit you the best.

02 Fabric selection & Measurements

設定西服樣式後，依照客戶的需求與喜好，挑選世界各地優秀的布料與配件，並提供介紹與體驗；接著進行完整的尺寸測量，記錄所有身型特徵與體型判別

Gaute offers an extensive range of materials, with our bespoke cloth swatch collection exceeding more than 2000 samples. Moreover, a full set of measurements are taken during the first consultation to ascertain shape, proportion, posture and balance. A paper pattern is then drafted, unique to the customer.

03 Striking pattern, cutting ＆ Basting

依據所測量的數據製作版型，並針對不同體型的顧客做紙型補正，每件都是獨立紙型，根據不同體型微調；接著將布料按所製出的紙型進行剪裁，以純手工的方式縫合結構試穿與修樣

The unique measurements of every customer are captured by a paper drafted pattern. All garment fabrics are matched in pattern as closely as possible at the collar, shoulder and welts in order to create a visually flowing and coherent piece. And all the pieces of a suit are initially “basted” together with white thread for the fitting process.

04 Fitting ＆ re-cutting:

當顧客套上毛胚樣衣，直接在人體作微調；所有樣式、比例、線條和細節都再三琢磨，以達到完全量身訂製的效果；並且再次修正做出最細膩的尺寸調整

At the fitting process, the customer and fitter are able to gauge, adjust and review the conception of the garment’s final fit as well as any corresponding work that is essential to achieve those results. After the fitting stages, the garment is completely broken down again in order to incorporate the necessary changes.

05 Sewing process

手工西裝之所以為『活』的西裝，其精髓在於全毛襯結構。裁縫師傅將一針一線地結合馬毛、羊毛等數十種配件，並固定在表布上；最後縫上手工扣眼，這些工序都比機縫耗費多出數百倍的工時，然而，這就是手工

During the sewing process, there is a considerable amount of pressing that is required to manipulate the fabric into a fully live and three-dimensional part of the whole garment. Familiarity of the fabric and experience is crucial here to attain the right curves and shape. The traditional materials of horsehair together with the suiting fabric provide the structure which will not only last but also conform fully to the client’s body.

06 Ironing ＆ Label

屆縫製完成之時，將進行最後整燙；手工西裝最忌燙平，而是透過熨燙呈現布料與毛襯最佳的質感，使西服外觀自然流暢最後，我們將Gaute織標縫製於西服內部，是Gaute對於出品的品質保證，也是這套西服的永久保固

Finally the smart label will placed on the lining and outside of the garment and signed Gaute.
